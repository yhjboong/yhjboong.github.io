Hi! My name is Dan and I am a sophomore majoring computer science at the University of Notre Dame.

About me

Coder, Technophile, Traveler, Gym Rat

I was born and raised in Seoul, South Korea. My family consists of my father, mother, and older sister. My sister pursued her Masters in Information Science at the University of Michigan, leading to a friendly family rivalry as I chose the University of Notre Dame for my studies.

During the online curriculum of the 2020 academic year, I decided to fulfill my military service in South Korea, serving 18 months in the Reconnaissance Troop, 55th Brigade, 22nd Infantry Division. It was a challenging yet honor-filled duty.

After my military discharge, I embarked on a European adventure with my best friend, exploring cities like Napoli, Barcelona, Seville, Geneva, and Paris. This journey was an eye-opening experience, widening my perspective on the world.

Starting my freshman year at Notre Dame, I was fortunate to be assigned to O'Neill Family Hall, quickly forming a bond with my dorm mates who became like family, especially important as my own family was far away in South Korea.

Experience

Quantum AI

Software Engineer Intern

I had the privilege of working with Quantum AI, a prominent player in the finance sector specializing in refining unstructured data through AI technology. At Quantum AI, we leveraged our proprietary natural language processing engine, "Cherry," to analyze diverse data types, including voice and text data sourced from public agencies.

I've established a robust local Asterisk PBX configuration within a Linux environment, leveraging Docker containers for enhanced performance and manageability. Integration of a Python-based NLP engine proficient in both text-to-speech (TTS) and speech-to-text (STT) capabilities enriches communication functionalities.

To ensure scalability and efficiency, I've devised a multi-threaded server infrastructure capable of handling high volumes of client contacts. Utilizing a load balancer effectively distributes queries across multiple servers, optimizing response times.

Additionally, I've developed an intuitive administrator-side application. This tool simplifies the management of customer service representatives, streamlines data analysis processes, and provides actionable insights to facilitate informed decision-making within the organization.

Garbage Collector

Software Engineer Intern

In my role, I spearheaded the development of a REST API for a Spring project utilizing Kotlin. This initiative was pivotal in launching a new iteration of our platform, facilitating the connection between waste disposal companies and business clientele seeking disposal solutions.

With meticulous attention to detail, I crafted comprehensive QA sheets to identify the requisite tools for our clients. Rigorous testing of the API using Postman ensured its robustness and reliability, guaranteeing a seamless user experience.

Beyond my technical responsibilities, I actively engaged in team meetings, offering valuable insights that influenced our service evolution and refined our business model.

Republic of Korea Army

Sergeant (Reconnaissance Assault Soldier)

Entrusted with a pivotal role in reconnaissance at the DMZ Guard Post in South Korea, I ensured the security and integrity of the border. My duties included coordinating weekly search missions and conducting K2C1 rifle shooting exercises. These tasks required seamless collaboration with individuals from diverse backgrounds, fostering an environment of respect and teamwork.

Recognizing the importance of efficient management, I leveraged Excel to automate our troop's vacation scheduling. This initiative streamlined procedures and ensured that everyone could secure their preferred time off, enhancing overall operational efficiency.

My time in the military, where I served in a multicultural setting, further influenced my viewpoint on leadership and cooperation by teaching me the importance of discipline, adaptation, and resilience. These experiences have given me a distinct perspective on the value of diversity and inclusivity in the tech field. They have instilled in me a deep appreciation for the diverse perspectives and skills that individuals from different backgrounds bring to the table. I firmly believe that embracing diversity not only fosters innovation but also promotes a more inclusive and equitable workplace environment.
